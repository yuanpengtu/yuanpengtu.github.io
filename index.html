Yuanpeng Tu



I am a first-year PhD student in CVAIL Lab of department of computer science, The University of Hong Kong fortunately supervised by Prof.Hengshuang Zhao.


Prior to this, I received my Master and Bachelor degree from Tongji University fortunately supervised by Prof.Cairong Zhao.


My current research mainly focuses on robust learning (i.e., noisy labels), open-world/zero-shot perception, and label-efficient learning. Feel free to shoot an email to discuss with me if you have any idea in these areas.
